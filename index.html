Researcher, Educator, Engineer

I am an Assistant Professor of Instruction in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Rochester, where I also earned my M.S. and Ph.D. in 2018 and 2024, respectively.

My research centers on digital image processing, with an emphasis on digital forensics and information security. I have developed novel image-based communication methods that prioritize privacy and security through signal processing techniques. My work has been published in the IEEE Transactions on Image Processing.

At the University of Rochester, I teach Senior Design, Wireless Communications, and Signals. I also co-teach Strategic Entrepreneurship and Innovation for Engineers with Roberto Colangelo, Director of the Ain Center for Entrepreneurship and Innovation.
