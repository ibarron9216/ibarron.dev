About Me

Researcher, Educator, Engineer

Dr. Irving Barron is an Assistant Professor of Instruction in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Rochester. His research spans digital image processing, information security, and privacy-preserving communication. He is passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical foundations and practical engineering, bringing real-world applications into the classroom to inspire the next generation of engineers.
