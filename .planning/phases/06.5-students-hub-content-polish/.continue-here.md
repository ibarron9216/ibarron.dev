<decisions_made>

1. **Accordion Structure for Impact & Outcomes** — Chose accordion pattern to let students jump directly to outcomes they care about (scholarships/jobs don't have to scroll past research)
   - Reason: UX improvement, allows selective content consumption

2. **5 Accordion Sections** — Decided on Graduate Programs, Career, Research, Other, What Students Say
   - Reason: Covers all major student outcome categories while keeping "Other" flexible

3. **Unicode Chevron Indicator (▼)** — Simple, clean, rotates 180° on expand
   - Reason: Fits design system, far superior to previous gradient pseudo-element approach

4. **Content Fully Hidden (display: none)** — Not just overflow-cut
   - Reason: Only section header visible when closed, matches user's intent

5. **"What Students Say" Title** — Selected from alternatives after user feedback
   - Reason: Clear, simple, immediately conveys student testimonials/quotes
